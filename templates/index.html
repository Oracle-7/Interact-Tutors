About Us

Interact Tutors is a free online tutoring service targeted towards elementary, midle, and high school students started by members of Monta Vista Interact, a volunteer club committed to providing Service over Self. As students face a significant educational gap during quarantine due to the lack of their usual teacher and peer support, we aim to solidify students' learning foundations and support their academic journeys through these tough months.

We offer tutors for a wide variety of both school-taught and extracurricular academic subjects in different fields, ranging from language arts to biology to machine learning, and will help you with your specific needs, whether your child needs help on a current academic subject or needs a headstart on a subject they're planning to take. Our tutors are high-performing students from Monta Vista High School who have chosen to volunteer in support of our cause and possess significant experience in the subjects they choose to tutor as well as a genuine desire to help others in the process.

Our classes are all delivered through Zoom, a free and widely-used online conferencing service that provides various tools and functions to allow for smooth, uninterrupted lessons.
